Put all your training images WITH vests in this folder.

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png

Example filenames:
- person_with_vest_001.jpg
- vest_image_1.png
- training_vest.jpg

IMPORTANT: See ../LABELING_GUIDE.md for what counts as "vest"
- High-visibility safety vests (orange, yellow, green)
- Must have reflective strips or clear vest characteristics
- Should be clearly recognizable as a safety vest

Make sure images clearly show vests being worn.
